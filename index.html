About Me

Hi!

I'm Joseph, a software developer with an interest in and experience with design, web dev, API technologies, and HR platforms

I enjoy both the behind-the-scenes and front-end aspects of web design: trying to find the most efficient way to implement a client's vision while also making sure the front-facing site looks and works great.

I currently have experience coding in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Python, and SQL. I am constantly looking to improve my experience in these languages, as well as learn new ones.

I know my way around quite a few graphic design tools, such as Sketch, Pixelmator, and Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator. My favorite has to be Sketch, just for its simplicity and ease of use.

If you have any questions about me, feel free to send me an email at joseph@lunine.net!
